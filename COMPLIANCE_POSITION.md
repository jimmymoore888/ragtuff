# RagTuff — Compliance Position

## Purpose
This document defines the compliance posture, operational boundaries, and intended use of RagTuff. It is provided for legal, procurement, and risk assessment purposes.

## System Classification
RagTuff is a **decision-support and analytical system**. It provides modeling, evaluation, and consequence analysis to support human decision-making. RagTuff is **advisory-only** by design.

## No Execution Authority
RagTuff does not execute actions, initiate transactions, issue commands, or control external systems. All outputs are informational and analytical. Final decisions and any resulting actions are performed exclusively by the user or the user’s independent systems.

## Non-Custodial Design
RagTuff does not:
- Store or custody funds, assets, private keys, seed phrases, or credentials  
- Possess signing authority  
- Act as an intermediary for transactions  
- Control or access user accounts, wallets, or infrastructure  

All assets and execution capabilities remain entirely under user control at all times.

## No Fiduciary Role
RagTuff does not provide fiduciary services, discretionary management, or automated decision-making. It does not act on behalf of users, nor does it assume responsibility for outcomes. Use of RagTuff does not create a fiduciary, agency, or advisory relationship.

## Human Agency and Responsibility
RagTuff preserves full human agency. Users retain sole responsibility for interpreting outputs, making decisions, and executing any actions. RagTuff provides analysis and modeling only.

## Intended Use
RagTuff is intended for lawful civilian and enterprise use, including strategic planning, financial and operational risk analysis, adversarial modeling, and consequence evaluation under uncertainty.

## Summary
RagTuff is:
- Advisory-only  
- Non-custodial  
- Non-executing  
- Non-fiduciary  

It supports decision-making without assuming authority or control.
